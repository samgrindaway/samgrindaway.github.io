About Grind Away Coffee

Grind Away aspires to bring a new coffee experience to the South West  by merging the convenience of mobile services with the warmth, charm and quality of a local Brick & Mortar Coffee shop.

 In a world that’s always moving, our mobile coffee shops are a welcoming and convenient place where people can slow down, take a break from the demands of life, savour a special moment and re-fuel their day. 

We believe there is a comforting and worthwhile pause waiting for all of us in a good cup of coffee and we’re excited to share this experience with guests.  We strive to earn the trust of our guests, employees and partners, while enriching the human experience in our community.
